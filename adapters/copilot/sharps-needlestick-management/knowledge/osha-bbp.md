# United States — OSHA Bloodborne Pathogens Standard (1910.1030) + Needlestick Act

**Fragment ID:** `KB-REG-OSHA-BBP`
**What this is:** a copyright-safe **paragraph → duty → artifact** citation map for
OSHA 29 CFR 1910.1030 (the Bloodborne Pathogens standard) and the Needlestick
Safety and Prevention Act 2000 that amended it. The discipline it forces is
**engineering and work-practice controls first (sharps with engineered
sharps-injury protections, safer devices), with PPE and post-exposure response as
the residual lines** — the hierarchy of controls applied to bloodborne-pathogen
exposure.
**What this is NOT:** a reproduction of the standard text. Cite the paragraph
topics only — never paste the rule wording (user holds the licensed copy).
