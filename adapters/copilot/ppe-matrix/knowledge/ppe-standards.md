# PPE standards reference — selection by standard, with source+year

**Fragment ID:** `KB-DATA-PPE-STANDARDS`
**Discipline:** PPE is the **last** line of defence (`KB-SNIP-HOC`) — propose it only
after higher-order controls. When PPE is specified, cite the **conformity standard**
for the user's jurisdiction with `source`+`year`; do not assert protection without it.

> Volatile: true · last_reviewed: 2026-05-15 (review window 180 days, D-05).

---

## Selection rule

1. Confirm higher-order controls have been applied/justified (`KB-SNIP-HOC`).
2. Specify the PPE *type* against the named hazard.
3. Cite the conformity standard for the jurisdiction.
