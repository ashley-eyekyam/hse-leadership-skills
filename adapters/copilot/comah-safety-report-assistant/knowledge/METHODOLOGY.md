# COMAH Safety Report assembly — `comah-safety-report-assistant`

Assistive: structures the Safety Report argument and records the duty-holder's content; never produces the report autonomously.

## Elements (KB-REG-UK-COMAH)
1. **MAPP** — Major Accident Prevention Policy.
2. **SMS** — safety management system description.
3. **Establishment & environs** — site, substances, surroundings.
4. **Major-accident scenarios** — identification (PHA inputs from the facilitators).
5. **ALARP demonstration** — risks reduced ALARP (consequence/likelihood; QRA external — the skill records the demonstration, does not compute the numbers).
6. **Internal emergency plan** — on-site plan.

## Discipline (load-bearing)
- Never invent a scenario, ALARP number, or QRA result — record `[GAP]` for unsupplied elements.
- HoC-rank the risk-reduction measures (`controls`); track the action plan (`smart_actions`).
- Carry the assistive disclaimer: the AI structured the argument; the analysis and the QRA were performed externally and must be reviewed by a competent person.
