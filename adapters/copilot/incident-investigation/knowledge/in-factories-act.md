## Legacy-first & transition flag

- **Default:** cite the legacy Factories-Act / state-rule obligation the regulator
  accepts today.
- **OSH Code note:** The Occupational Safety, Health and Working Conditions Code, 2020
  consolidates the Factories Act among other labour statutes, but is **not uniformly
  in force** — most states have not notified their OSH Rules; legacy filings remain
  valid under savings provisions. Always append the transition note from the relevant
  `KB-REG-IN-STATEFORMS` row's `osh_transition` field; never present the OSH Code as
  the live filing regime unless the user's state has notified.

## Mandatory state detection

No India-facing skill may cite a specific form before resolving the state. See
`KB-REG-IN-STATEFORMS` (topic `state-detection`): ask explicitly → may infer from a
supplied site address but **confirm before citing** → else fall back to "Unknown".

**No national form number is ever cited.** Forms are state-prescribed strings only.
