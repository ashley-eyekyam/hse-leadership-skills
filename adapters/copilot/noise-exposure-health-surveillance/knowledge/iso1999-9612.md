# ISO 1999:2013 + ISO 9612:2009 тАФ NIHL estimation + occupational-noise measurement (methodтЖТartifact map)

**Fragment ID:** `KB-STD-ISO1999-9612`
**What this is:** a copyright-safe **method map** for ISO 1999:2013 (estimation of
noise-induced hearing loss) and ISO 9612:2009 (determination of occupational noise
exposure тАФ the measurement/sampling method). It grounds a noise/health-surveillance
skill with the dosimetry method and the NIHL-estimation basis.
**What this is NOT:** a reproduction of either standard. The action-level comparison
runs against the cited 85 / 90 dBA thresholds carried on `KB-REG-OSHA1910-95`; **no
dosimetry is computed by the skill** тАФ the measured TWA is a transcribed input. Cite the
standard number and the method topics only.
