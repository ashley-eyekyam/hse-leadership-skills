# Methodology — principal-hazard management plan (assistive)

Extends the **B1 risk-assessment** HIRA structure + builds on **`icmm-critical-control-management`** (D-M4) for the critical-control linkage.

## Steps
1. **Intake the named principal hazard** from the mining taxonomy.
2. **HIRA + risk-rate** on the org matrix (`risk_matrix`, D-02 bands).
3. **Build the control suite** hierarchy-ranked (`controls` / `KB-SNIP-HOC`), critical controls flagged; a PPE/admin-only treatment of a principal hazard is escalated/justified.
4. **Link critical controls to ICMM CCM** — carry over the performance + verification from `icmm-critical-control-management`.
5. **Monitoring** — owner + due date (`smart_actions`).
6. **`[GAP]` not a fabricated value** — record `[GAP]` for any un-supplied engineering value (no-fabrication, D-05a).

Assistive: this structures the multidisciplinary workshop; the mine team owns the judgement.
