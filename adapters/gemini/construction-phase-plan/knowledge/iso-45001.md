ISO 45001 is the cross-cutting OH&S backbone nearly every skill leans on,
**independent of jurisdiction** — a HIRA is grounded in 6.1.2 whether the site is
in Chennai, Houston, or Aberdeen. Apply it alongside the one statutory jurisdiction
fragment a skill reads (see `../regulatory/`).

hierarchy of controls (`KB-SNIP-HOC`). The compliance-register clauses (6.1.3 / 9.1.2-equivalent)
