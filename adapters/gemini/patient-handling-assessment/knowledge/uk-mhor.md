# United Kingdom — Manual Handling Operations Regulations 1992 (MHOR)

**Fragment ID:** `KB-REG-UK-MHOR`
**What this is:** a copyright-safe **regulation → duty → artifact** citation map
for the Manual Handling Operations Regulations 1992 (MHOR), the binding UK regime
for manual-handling risk in healthcare patient handling. The discipline it forces
is the **avoid → assess → reduce** order, with the **TILE** assessment factors
(Task, Individual, Load, Environment) and a move toward **zero / minimal manual
lifting** of people.
**What this is NOT:** a reproduction of the regulation text. Cite the regulation
numbers and the TILE structure only — never paste the wording.

> Source: Manual Handling Operations Regulations 1992 (SI 1992/2793, as amended) + HSE guidance L23 — citation map · Year: 2026 · Reviewed: 2026-06-23 · Volatile: false (long-standing regulation).
