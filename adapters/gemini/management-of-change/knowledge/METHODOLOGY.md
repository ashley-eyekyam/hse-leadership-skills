# Management of Change + PSSR — `management-of-change`

Grounds in `KB-STD-PSM` (elements (l) MoC + (i) PSSR). The **PSSR is a hard pre-start-up gate** (B6 criteria-as-runtime-gate).

## Steps
1. **Specify the change** exactly. Permanent or temporary (temporary → expiry condition/date, flagged if absent).
2. **Technical basis** — the engineering/operational rationale.
3. **Change risk assessment** — hazards the change introduces, scored `risk_matrix` (initial vs residual), controls HoC-ranked (`controls`).
4. **PSSR checklist** — PSI updated, procedures updated, training done, RA controls in place, equipment as-designed. **No start-up authorization until every item passes.**
5. **Document/training updates** before start-up.
6. **Track** the action plan with `smart_actions`.

## Discipline (load-bearing)
- The PSSR is a GATE: an open item blocks start-up authorization — never waved through.
- A temporary change without an expiry is flagged.
