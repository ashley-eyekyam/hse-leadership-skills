# Rail CSM-RA — Reg (EU) 402/2013 (retained EU law in GB) risk-assessment map

**Fragment ID:** `KB-REG-CSM-RA`
**What this is:** a copyright-safe **significance-test → risk-acceptance →
independent-assessment map** for the Common Safety Method for Risk Evaluation and
Assessment (CSM-RA) — grounding `rail-safety-management-system` (RAIL-01) and
`safety-authorisation` (RAIL-02). It names the significance test, the three
risk-acceptance principles and the independent AsBo role.
**What this is NOT:** a reproduction of Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) No
402/2013. Cite the regulation number and the method topics only.

> Source: Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) No 402/2013 (Common Safety Method for Risk Evaluation and Assessment); retained EU law in GB from 1 Jan 2021 — citation map · Year: 2026 · Reviewed: 2026-06-23 · Volatile: false.

CSM-RA is the harmonised risk-assessment method for significant changes to the GB
rail system. It is **retained EU law in GB from 1 January 2021** and still applies.
It grounds the change-risk-assessment limb of a rail SMS / authorisation artifact.

## Element → method → artifact it grounds

| Element | Method (paraphrased) | Artifact it grounds |
|---|---|---|
| **Significance test** | Decide whether a change is *significant* (safety relevance / novelty / complexity / monitoring / reversibility / additionality) | significance determination record |
| **Three risk-acceptance principles** | Demonstrate risk acceptability via (1) codes of practice, (2) reference systems, or (3) explicit risk estimation | risk-acceptance argument |
| **Independent AsBo** (Assessment Body) | An independent assessment body assesses the correct application of CSM-RA for a significant change | AsBo assessment report |
| Hazard record | Maintain a hazard record through the change lifecycle | hazard record |

## How the skill uses this fragment
- RAIL-01 grounds the SMS's change-management element on the CSM-RA significance
  test + risk-acceptance principles + AsBo independence.
- RAIL-02 references the same method for change-related authorisation evidence —
  it does not rebuild the SMS (RAIL-01 owns it).
- Operator-specific change details and risk figures are `[GAP]` until supplied.
