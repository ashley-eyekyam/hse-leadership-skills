# United Kingdom — Offshore Installations (Safety Case) Regulations 2015 + SEMS map

**Fragment ID:** `KB-REG-OFFSHORE-SCR`
**What this is:** a copyright-safe **duty → safety-case-element map** for the
Offshore Installations (Offshore Safety Directive) (Safety Case etc.) Regulations
2015 (SCR 2015) — the safety case acceptance regime for UK offshore installations,
the major-accident-hazard demonstration, and the Safety & Environmental
Management System (SEMS) duties that ground an offshore operation.
**What this is NOT:** a reproduction of SCR 2015, the OSD, or HSE/OPRED guidance.
Cite the Regulation/Schedule numbers and the duty topics only — never paste the
statutory wording.

> Source: Offshore Installations (Offshore Safety Directive) (Safety Case etc.) Regulations 2015 (SI 2015/398) + Offshore Safety Directive 2013/30/EU + SEMS framing — citation map · Year: 2026 · Reviewed: 2026-06-21 · Volatile: true (statutory status + competent-authority guidance drift; refresh on review).

SCR 2015 implements the EU Offshore Safety Directive for UK offshore oil & gas.
It requires a duty-holder to prepare a **safety case** accepted by the competent
authority, demonstrating that major accident hazards are identified and risks are
reduced ALARP, and to operate a SEMS. It grounds offshore safety-case, SIMOPS and
major-accident skills.

## Topic → duty → safety-case element it grounds

| Topic | Duty (paraphrased) | Safety-case element it grounds |
|---|---|---|
| Operator / owner safety case | Prepare a safety case for acceptance | the full safety case submission |
| Major accident hazards (MAH) | Identify MAH and major-accident scenarios | MAH identification (PHA / HAZID inputs) |
| Risk reduction | Demonstrate risks reduced ALARP | ALARP demonstration |
| Safety & environmental critical elements | Identify SECEs + performance standards | SECE register + performance standards |
| Verification | Independent verification scheme for SECEs | verification scheme + verifier findings |
| Emergency response | On-installation + combined emergency arrangements | emergency response plan |
| SEMS | Operate a safety & environmental management system | the SEMS document set |

## How the skill uses this fragment
- An offshore skill resolves the safety-case element first, then records the
  duty-holder's content — it never produces the safety case autonomously; QRA /
  consequence modelling and the ALARP numbers are external inputs.
- SECE performance standards are stated as the *test* the duty-holder evidences;
  a missing element is recorded as `[GAP]`, never invented.
