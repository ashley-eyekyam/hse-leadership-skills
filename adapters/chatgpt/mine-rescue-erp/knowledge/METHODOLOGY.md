# Methodology — mine-rescue emergency response plan

Extends the **B8 sop-writer** emergency-procedure document over **B1** scenario hazard-ranking.

## Steps
1. **Intake the actual mine + scenarios + rescue resources** — realism over template.
2. **Scenario-rank** each emergency on the org matrix (`risk_matrix`).
3. **Build the mobilisation sequence** — rescue team -> station -> mutual-aid -> NDMA/DGMS alignment, with **credible timing** (not aspirational).
4. **Schedule drills** with owner + date (`smart_actions`).
5. **Cite the Mines Act/DGMS emergency-preparedness duty** (`KB-REG-IN-MINES-ACT`).
6. **De-identify first** — incident/casualty data carries injured-miner PII; scrub to role labels and suppress <5 cells before drafting.

Every conclusion traces to evidence; benchmark figures carry source+year or `[GAP]`.
