# Methodology — mine ventilation / strata-control / blasting plan

Extends the **B1 risk-assessment** multi-agent pattern (Researcher + Risk-Scorer + Drafter + Critic) over the **B8 sop-writer** plan-document structure.

## Steps
1. **Intake the actual mine + plan parameters** — site-specific; reject a generic template.
2. **Hazard-rank** each principal hazard on the org matrix (`risk_matrix`, 5x5, D-02 bands).
3. **Build the control suite** hierarchy-ranked (`controls` / `KB-SNIP-HOC`); flag the **critical controls** (ICMM-CCM); a PPE/admin-only treatment of a principal hazard is escalated or justified, never accepted.
4. **Cite the Mines Act/DGMS plan duty** (`KB-REG-IN-MINES-ACT`) — the plans are site documents, not numbered forms.
5. **SMART actions** — owner + due date on every follow-up (`smart_actions`).
6. **Benchmark figures** carry source+year (`KB-DATA-MINING-HAZARDS`) or `[GAP]`.

Every conclusion traces to evidence; no vague control; no PPE-only treatment of a principal hazard.
