## Usage note

Recordable-injury definitions (1904) drive the incident-rate benchmarks — see
`../data-points/incident-rates-benchmarks.md` (`KB-DATA-TRIR-BENCHMARKS`). The 1904.29
"privacy concern case" rule is referenced by the de-id checklist (A5). Ground
management-system structure in `KB-STD-ISO45001`; rank controls via `KB-SNIP-HOC`.
