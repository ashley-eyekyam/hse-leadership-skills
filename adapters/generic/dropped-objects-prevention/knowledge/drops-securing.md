## Instruction — the dropped-objects control order

1. **Eliminate (primary).** Remove items from height; design out the dropped-object
   source; do the task at ground level where reasonably practicable.
2. **Substitute / engineer (reliable securing).** Primary fixing + secondary
   retention (tethering/lanyards) to a reliable-securing standard; barriers/nets.
3. **Administrative.** DROPS survey + register, inspection regime, exclusion/red
   zones below at-height work, permit control.
4. **PPE (residual only).** Hard hats for persons who must be in the area —
   supplement the survey/securing/exclusion controls, never replace them.

## The gate (reject these)
- A treatment that leads with "hard hats below" instead of survey + reliable
  securing + exclusion zones → **reject** (PPE-led).
- An at-height item with no recorded securing standard → **reject**.
- An impact-energy band asserted with hard-coded licensed thresholds → **reject**;
  record the user's DROPS band, leave thresholds `[GAP]`.

## How the skill uses this fragment
MAR-02 ranks every dropped-object control through this order and rejects a
PPE-for-those-below treatment.
