HAZOP is an **assistive** method: the live multidisciplinary team performs the
engineering judgement; the skill structures the study and records the team's
findings. It grounds `hazop-facilitator`.

## Assistive discipline (load-bearing)
- The skill **records the team's** deviations, causes, consequences, and safeguard
  judgements ‚Äî it does **not** invent deviations the team did not raise.
- Where the team has not supplied a value, the worksheet records `[GAP]` ‚Äî
  never a fabricated engineering value.
- The report names the **team participants & competencies** (the assistive
  evidence) and carries the assistive disclaimer.
