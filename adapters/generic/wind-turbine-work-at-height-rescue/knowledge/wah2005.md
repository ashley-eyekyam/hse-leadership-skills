# Work at Height Regulations 2005 (SI 2005/735) — planning & hierarchy duty map

**Fragment ID:** `KB-REG-WAH2005`
**What this is:** a copyright-safe **planning → hierarchy → inspection map** for the
Work at Height Regulations 2005 — grounding `wind-turbine-work-at-height-rescue`
(REN-01) and `weather-dynamic-risk-assessment` (REN-03). It names reg 4 planning
(incl. emergencies & rescue), the reg 6 avoid→prevent→mitigate hierarchy, the reg 7
collective-before-personal rule and reg 12 inspection.
**What this is NOT:** a reproduction of SI 2005/735. Cite the regulation numbers and
the duty topics only.

> Source: Work at Height Regulations 2005 (SI 2005/735), under the Health and Safety at Work etc. Act 1974 — citation map · Year: 2026 · Reviewed: 2026-06-23 · Volatile: false.

The Work at Height Regulations 2005 set the duties for planning, organising and
carrying out work at height in GB. They ground the work-at-height limb of a wind /
renewables artifact, including the mandatory rescue planning.

## Clause → duty → artifact it grounds

| Regulation | Duty (paraphrased) | Artifact it grounds |
|---|---|---|
| **Reg 4** | Plan, organise and supervise work at height — **including planning for emergencies and rescue** | work-at-height plan + rescue plan |
| **Reg 6** | Avoid work at height where reasonably practicable → prevent falls (collective) → mitigate/arrest the distance & consequences | control-selection hierarchy |
| **Reg 7** | Select work equipment giving **collective protection priority over personal** protection | equipment-selection record |
| **Reg 8 + Schedules 2–6** | Requirements for specific equipment (guardrails, working platforms, nets/airbags, personal fall protection, ladders) | equipment requirements |
| **Reg 12** | Inspect work-at-height equipment at suitable intervals | inspection regime |

## The control order (avoid → collective → personal)
Falls are controlled by **avoiding work at height → collective fall-prevention →
personal fall-arrest** — and **reg 4 makes rescue planning part of the work, not an
afterthought.** A WAH plan with no tested rescue (see `KB-SNIP-RESCUE-PLAN`) is the
indefensible output this map rejects.

## How the skill uses this fragment
- REN-01 grounds the WAH plan + mandatory rescue plan on reg 4 + the reg 6/7
  hierarchy; REN-03 grounds the dynamic point-of-work re-assessment that holds/stops
