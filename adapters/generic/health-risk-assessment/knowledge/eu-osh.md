## Usage note

The Art. 6 "general principles of prevention" are the EU statement of the hierarchy
of controls — apply `KB-SNIP-HOC` and flag any avoidance-of-risk-at-source omission.
For the binding form/return, cite the relevant **member-state transposition**, not
the directive. Ground management-system structure in `KB-STD-ISO45001`.
