# Bowtie / barrier method (CCPS/EI; ICMM CCM) — `bowtie-builder`

Assistive: structures the bowtie and records the team's barrier judgements; never declares a barrier effective without evidence.

## Steps
1. **Hazard** — name the specific energy/material.
2. **Top event** — the loss-of-control / release point.
3. **Threats** (left) — one per line; **consequences** (right) phrased '[Damage] due to [Event]'.
4. **Barriers** — preventive (threat side) + mitigative (consequence side). For each: effective · independent · auditable + a **performance standard**. No evidence → `[GAP]`, not 'effective'.
5. **Critical controls (CCM)** — for a barrier that prevents/mitigates a major accident: performance standard + verification activity + owner.
6. **HoC-rank** barriers (`controls.rank_controls`); flag administrative/PPE-only barrier sets. **Risk-band** (`risk_matrix`); **track** verification actions (`smart_actions`).

## Assistive discipline (load-bearing)
- Never declare a barrier effective without the team's performance-standard evidence — record `[GAP]`.
- Name the **team participants & competencies**; carry the assistive disclaimer.
