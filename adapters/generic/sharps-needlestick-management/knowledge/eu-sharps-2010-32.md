# European Union / United Kingdom — Sharps in healthcare (Directive 2010/32/EU + UK Sharps Regs 2013)

**Fragment ID:** `KB-REG-EU-SHARPS-2010-32`
**What this is:** a copyright-safe **clause → duty → artifact** citation map for
Council Directive 2010/32/EU (implementing the framework agreement on prevention
of sharps injuries in the hospital and healthcare sector) and its UK
transposition, the Health and Safety (Sharps Instruments in Healthcare)
Regulations 2013, read with COSHH. The discipline it forces is **eliminate
unnecessary sharps, then use safer engineered devices and safe procedures, with
PPE and post-exposure response as residual lines.**
**What this is NOT:** a reproduction of the directive / regulation text. Cite the
clause topics only; for the binding obligation cite the member-state transposition.

## Clause topic → duty → artifact it grounds

| Clause topic (cite, don't paste) | Duty (paraphrased) | Artifact it grounds |
|---|---|---|
| Risk assessment | Assess where sharps are used and the exposure risk | the sharps risk assessment |
| Eliminate / avoid unnecessary sharps | Remove sharps where not needed; ban recapping | the sharps-elimination record |
| Safer engineered devices | Provide medical devices with safety-engineered protection | the safer-device specification |
| Safe procedures + safe disposal | Define use, handling and immediate point-of-use disposal | the safe-procedure + disposal plan |
| Information, training & PPE | Train staff; provide PPE as a residual control | the training + PPE record |
| Reporting + post-exposure response | Report every injury; provide post-exposure prophylaxis | the incident report + PEP record |
