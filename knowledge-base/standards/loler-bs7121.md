# Lifting operations — LOLER 1998 + BS 7121 (lift-planning / appointed-person / SWL map)

**Fragment ID:** `KB-REG-LOLER-BS7121`
**What this is:** a copyright-safe **lift-planning duty → method map** for the Lifting
Operations and Lifting Equipment Regulations 1998 (LOLER) Regulation 8 (planning,
organisation, supervision) and Regulation 9 (thorough examination), plus the BS 7121
*Safe Use of Cranes* code-of-practice framework — lift categorisation, the
appointed-person role, the safe-working-load / utilisation method, and the
exclusion-zone method.
**What this is NOT:** a reproduction of LOLER, the L113 ACOP, or BS 7121 text, and it
carries no rated-capacity tables. Cite the Regulation/clause numbers and the method
topics only — never paste the regulation or standard wording, and never invent an SWL.

> Source: Lifting Operations and Lifting Equipment Regulations 1998 (SI 1998/2307) + HSE L113 ACOP + BS 7121 *Safe Use of Cranes* — citation map · Year: 2026 · Reviewed: 2026-06-22 · Volatile: false (stable regs/standard; refresh on review).

LOLER governs the planning, supervision and examination of lifting operations and
lifting equipment in Great Britain; BS 7121 is the recognised UK code of practice for
the safe use of cranes. Together they ground a lifting-plan skill with the
appointed-person framework and the categorise → plan → examine method, applied through
the hierarchy of controls.

## Duty / topic → clause → method it grounds

| Duty / topic | Clause | Method it grounds |
|---|---|---|
| Plan the lifting operation | LOLER Reg 8(1)(a) | every lift **properly planned by a competent person**; complex/tandem/blind lifts demand a written lift plan + contingency/abort criteria |
| Organise & supervise the lift | LOLER Reg 8(1)(b)–(c) | named roles (appointed person / crane operator / slinger-signaller) with a competence basis; the lift is supervised and safely carried out |
| Thorough examination of equipment | LOLER Reg 9 | in-date thorough-examination certificate for the crane and lifting accessories before reliance |
| Lift categorisation | BS 7121 | classify **basic / standard / complex** to set the planning depth (see `KB-DATA-LIFT-CATEGORIES` for the criteria + thresholds) |
| Appointed-person role | BS 7121 | the appointed person plans the lift, selects equipment, and takes overall control; standard/complex lifts require one to be named |
| SWL / utilisation | BS 7121 | check the **SWL at the working radius** from the manufacturer's rated-capacity chart against the load (incl. rigging); a utilisation above the safe threshold forces re-selection — the value is a transcribed input, never computed by the skill |
| Exclusion zone & proximity | BS 7121 | establish an exclusion zone for fall-of-load and slew; overhead power lines / adjacent structures / public interface are eliminated or physically controlled before signage/PPE |

## How the skill uses this fragment
- A lifting skill resolves the **lift category** first (BS 7121), then sets the
  planning depth and whether an appointed-person plan is mandatory.
- LOLER Reg 8 is the planning/supervision grounding; Reg 9 is the examination gate.
- SWL-at-radius and utilisation are stated as the **test** against the rated-capacity
  chart; an unconfirmed load weight or "should be fine" ground is a `[GAP]`, never
  assumed. PPE is never the primary control for a lift hazard (`KB-SNIP-HOC`).
