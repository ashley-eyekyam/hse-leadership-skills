## Psychosocial hazard categories (work-organisation lens)

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| Work design / organisation | excessive workload/pace, long/unsocial hours, low autonomy, unclear roles |
| Social factors at work | poor support, bullying/harassment, isolation, weak leadership |
| Work environment / equipment | unsafe/inadequate environment compounding strain |
| Change & job security | poorly managed change, insecurity, organisational instability |

## Control discipline

Apply `KB-SNIP-HOC`: psychosocial controls must be **work-design controls** (change
the work — workload, autonomy, role clarity, support), not individual-resilience-only
measures. Flag resilience-/EAP-only recommendations as lower-order (justify-or-escalate).
