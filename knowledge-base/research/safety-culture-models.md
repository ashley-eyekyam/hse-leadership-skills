# Safety culture & maturity models — background reference

**Fragment ID:** `KB-RES-SAFETY-CULTURE`
Background reference for safety-culture framing in audits, board reports, and
incident-investigation organisational-factor analysis.

> Source: recognised safety-culture / maturity-model literature (named below) · Year: 2026 · Reviewed: 2026-05-15 · Volatile: false.

## Widely-referenced models (named, not reproduced)

| Model | Core idea | Use in artifacts |
|---|---|---|
| Safety-culture maturity ladder (pathological → reactive → calculative → proactive → generative) | culture progresses through stages | audit maturity assessment; board narrative |
| Just Culture | distinguish human error / at-risk behaviour / reckless conduct; learn vs blame | incident-investigation; CAPA framing |
| Heinrich / accident-pyramid (and its modern critiques) | near-miss → incident relationship; treat the critique, not just the ratio | leading-indicator argument; caution against over-claiming ratios |
| Swiss-cheese / systemic models | latent organisational conditions align with active failures | incident-investigation organisational-factor analysis |

## Usage note

Use models to *frame* organisational factors and maturity — always tie back to
specific, evidenced findings (`KB-SNIP-DEFENSIBILITY`); do not present a model as a
finding in itself. Pair with `KB-STD-ISO45001` (9.2 audit, 10.2 incident).
