# United States / United Kingdom — Occupational noise exposure (OSHA 1910.95 + UK Noise Regs 2005 map)

**Fragment ID:** `KB-REG-OSHA1910-95`
**What this is:** a copyright-safe **threshold → duty → artifact map** for occupational
noise exposure: OSHA 29 CFR 1910.95 (the 85 dBA 8-hr TWA action level triggering a
hearing-conservation program, the 90 dBA PEL, audiometric testing + standard threshold
shift, hearing-protection provision, and training) with a UK jurisdiction row for the
Control of Noise at Work Regulations 2005 (80/85 dB(A) action values, 87 dB(A) limit
value).
**What this is NOT:** a reproduction of the regulation text or a dosimetry calculator.
The measured TWA is a **transcribed input** compared against these cited thresholds;
the skill performs no dosimetry computation. Cite the regulation + the named threshold.

> Source: OSHA 29 CFR 1910.95 (Occupational Noise Exposure) + UK Control of Noise at Work Regulations 2005 — citation map · Year: 2026 · Reviewed: 2026-06-22 · Volatile: false (stable thresholds; refresh on review).

This fragment grounds a noise-exposure-health-surveillance skill (MFG-03) with the
exposure thresholds and the hearing-conservation duty per jurisdiction, applied through
the noise-control hierarchy.

## Jurisdiction → threshold → duty/artifact it grounds

| Jurisdiction | Threshold (named) | Duty / artifact it grounds |
|---|---|---|
| US — OSHA 1910.95 | **85 dBA** 8-hr TWA action level | hearing-conservation program: monitoring, audiometric testing, hearing protection, training, recordkeeping |
| US — OSHA 1910.95 | **90 dBA** PEL (8-hr TWA) | mandatory feasible engineering/administrative controls; HPD where controls do not achieve the PEL |
| US — OSHA 1910.95 | **standard threshold shift (STS)** | audiometric follow-up + protective action on a confirmed STS |
| UK — Noise Regs 2005 | lower **80 dB(A)** / upper **85 dB(A)** action values | information/training + HPD availability (80); HPD zones + health surveillance (85) |
| UK — Noise Regs 2005 | **87 dB(A)** exposure limit value | must not be exceeded (accounting for HPD attenuation) |

## How the skill uses this fragment
- `noise-exposure-health-surveillance` resolves the jurisdiction, compares the
  **transcribed** LEX,8h against the cited action/limit thresholds above, and grounds
  the hearing-conservation/surveillance duty.
- The measurement method is `KB-STD-ISO1999-9612`; controls follow
  `KB-SNIP-NOISE-CONTROL-HIERARCHY` (source first, HPD last).
- A missing measured TWA is a `[GAP]`; audiometric results are health data (small-cell
  suppression, de-identification).
