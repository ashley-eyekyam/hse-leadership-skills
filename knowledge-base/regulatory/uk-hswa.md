# United Kingdom — Health & Safety at Work etc. Act 1974 (HSWA)

**Fragment ID:** `KB-REG-UK-HSWA`
**Jurisdiction:** United Kingdom (UK).

> Source: Health and Safety at Work etc. Act 1974, the Management of Health and Safety at Work Regulations 1999 (MHSWR), and the Construction (Design and Management) Regulations 2015 (CDM 2015) · Year: 2026 · Reviewed: 2026-06-16 · Volatile: false.

Statutory framing for UK HSE work. Topic map only — no reproduced statute text.

## Usage note

"Reasonably practicable" is the UK proportionality test; pair control selection with
`KB-SNIP-HOC` and justify any lower-order (admin/PPE-only) control against it.
Ground management-system structure in `KB-STD-ISO45001`.

For UK **construction** work, the CDM 2015 rows above frame the duty hierarchy a
RAMS/JSA discharges: a contractor's safe system of work (reg. 15) and the
principal-contractor's plan-manage-monitor duty (reg. 13) both roll up into the
**Construction Phase Plan** (reg. 12 / reg. 15(4)) — cite the regulation, never
reproduce statute text. (A dedicated CDM fragment may be split out later; for v1.0
the CDM rows live here under `KB-REG-UK-HSWA`.)
