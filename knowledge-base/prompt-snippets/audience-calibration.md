# Audience Calibration — pitch the artifact to its reader

**Fragment ID:** `KB-SNIP-AUDIENCE`
**Prompt text.** Calibrates depth, language, and emphasis to the named audience so a
board paper, a frontline toolbox talk, and a consultant's RAMS each land correctly.

> Source: master-plan audience model (M = manager/leadership · C = consultant/competent person · F = frontline/supervisor) · Year: 2026 · Reviewed: 2026-05-15 · Volatile: false.

---

## Instruction

Resolve the audience before drafting (it is part of intake; see `KB-SNIP-INTAKE`),
then calibrate:

| Audience | Pitch | Emphasis | Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| **M** — leadership / board | decision-grade, concise, risk-and-cost framed | residual risk, KPIs, accountability, owners & dates | jargon dumps, raw clause text |
| **C** — consultant / competent person | technical, fully cited, defensible | clause references, methodology, evidence trail | hand-waving, uncited assertions |
| **F** — frontline / supervisor | plain, actionable, task-specific | what to do, what to check, what to stop | abstraction, statute numbers without "so do X" |

Across all audiences: keep controls ranked (`KB-SNIP-HOC`), keep figures sourced
(`source`+`year`), and keep conclusions tied to named owners and dates.
