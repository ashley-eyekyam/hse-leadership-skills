  gated and signed.

the one-way / segregation control — no named driver, no date, no fitness detail circulated.

## Vehicle-pedestrian segregation (by design — the core value)

  gate (Elimination). The shared forklift route is physically separated by pedestrian barriers

  pushed up the hierarchy; the banksman is the LAST-resort residual control for the final reversing
  manoeuvre once the one-way system and segregation are designed in.
