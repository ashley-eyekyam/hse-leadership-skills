## Survey scope

This DROPS survey covers the Platform Bravo drilling derrick (monkeyboard to drill floor, UKCS).
The survey covers the at-height structures (derrick, monkeyboard and fingerboard, racking-board
lighting), classifies each object static vs dynamic, records the reliable-securing status, and sets
the exclusion zone below at-height work.

## Lead posture — reliable securing, not "hard hats below"

The proposed control reduced the treatment to "issue hard hats to the people working below the
derrick". That PPE-led posture is rejected: the lead controls are the DROPS survey, reliable
securing (primary fixing plus secondary retention) and the exclusion zone below at-height work; hard
hats are the residual for persons who must be in the area, never the headline control.

- Eliminate — remove non-essential items from the monkeyboard; design out the dropped-object source.
- Reliable securing (engineer) — primary fixing plus secondary retention (tethered lanyard) to the
  reliable-securing standard.
- Administrative — DROPS survey and register, weekly at-height inspection, exclusion/red zone below
  the derrick during tripping.
- PPE (residual) — hard hats for persons who must enter the drill floor, supplementing the securing
  and exclusion controls.
