# Pre-Construction Information (PCI) — Tower B brownfield refurbishment (CDM 2015 Regulation 4)

**Project:** Tower B brownfield refurbishment — strip out and reclad levels 3–5; the lower
floors (levels 0–2) remain occupied; a public footpath runs along the north boundary.
**Client:** the building owner (CDM 2015 Reg 4 duty-holder).
**Stage:** pre-tender. **Jurisdiction:** UK — CDM 2015 Reg 4 + HSE L153 Appendix 1.
**Distribution:** RESTRICTED — issued to designers and contractors being appointed or
considered (Reg 4(4)); not for unrestricted publication.

> De-identification ran first. Survey-derived occupiers appear by role label; an adjacent
> occupier's vulnerability is recorded at role level as a sequencing constraint, with no name,
> contact, or medical diagnosis circulated. The gap-action owners below are deliberate
> contractual appointments (a legitimate record), not leaked PII. The re-identification key is
> held separately.

## Client's Reg 4 duty (stated up front)
Under **CDM 2015 Regulation 4(4)** the client must provide this Pre-Construction Information
**as soon as practicable** to every designer and contractor appointed or being considered.
Under **Reg 4(5)/(6)** no construction phase may start without the arrangements and the
Construction Phase Plan. This pack is the information set, not the plan.

## 1. Project & client brief
Brownfield strip-out and recladding of levels 3–5 of an occupied building. Lower floors stay in
use throughout; the north public footpath sits beneath the cladding zone. The client is the
building owner and the Reg 4 duty-holder.
