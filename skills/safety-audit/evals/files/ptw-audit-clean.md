## Audit request (Q-Scope, Q-Crit, Q-Type)

- **Scope:** the permit-to-work (PTW) system at the Plant 3 maintenance shop — issuance,
  isolation, sign-off, close-out. **Boundary:** hot-work permits excluded.
- **Criteria (Q-Crit):** ISO 45001 clause 8.1 (operational control) + the org's own PTW
  procedure.
- **Audit type (Q-Type):** management-system audit.
- **Jurisdiction:** UK. **Matrix (Q-NCrate):** org 5x5.

## Evidence gathered (Q-Evid — scrubbed)

- **E-1** — a sample of 12 issued permits across 3 shifts (issuers/holders by role label).
  12/12 carry an authorised issuer role; 4/12 were signed off with **no recorded isolation
  check**; 2/12 lack the final close-out sign-back.
- **E-2** — the org PTW procedure (§3 issuance authorisation present).
- **E-3** — an isolation-register extract showing gaps against the 4 flagged permits.
- **E-4** — the PTW procedure §5: no independent isolation-verification step before sign-off.
- **E-5** — two interview summaries by role (Supervisor role; Issuer role).
- **E-6** — the PTW-authoriser training matrix (de-identified): all sampled issuers in date.
- **E-7** — direct observation of one permit issuance + shift handover.

## What the auditor needs

A clause-by-clause audit report: assess each criterion against the evidence, classify each
finding (conformity / NC-major / NC-minor / observation / opportunity), risk-rate every
nonconformity on the 5x5 matrix, and produce a HoC-ranked CAPA register with named (role)
owners and ISO dates, each action linked to its finding. Cite ISO 45001 clause 9.2 as the
method and clause 8.1 as the criteria.
