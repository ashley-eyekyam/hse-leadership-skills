# Construction Phase Plan — Tower B fit-out (CDM 2015 Regulation 12)

**Project:** Tower B fit-out — demolish level-3 partitions, then erect the steel frame and
install cladding to levels 3–5 of an occupied building with an adjacent public footpath.
**Contractor configuration:** Principal contractor appointed (Reg 12(2) duty owned).
**Notification:** NOTIFIABLE (>500 person-days) — F10 filed and displayed on site.
**Construction phase:** weeks 8–20. **Jurisdiction:** UK — CDM 2015 Reg 12 + HSE L153.

> fall-from-height near-miss is recorded at role level as evidence, with no name, date, or
> medical detail. The duty-holders below are the deliberate contractual appointments for this

## 1. Project description & programme
The fit-out proceeds in three phases — demolition (level-3 partitions) → steel-frame erection
(levels 3–5) → cladding installation (levels 3–5). The building remains occupied; a public
footpath runs adjacent to the cladding drop zone. Programme weeks 8–20, reviewed at each phase
transition.

## 3. Site rules
- No access to a work-at-height area without inspected edge protection in place.
- Permit-to-work for demolition, hot works, and any work near services.
- Exclusion zones segregate the public footpath from the cladding drop zone.
- All lifting under an appointed person; no lift over an occupied or public area.

## 4. Significant risks & controls by activity (hierarchy-ranked, residual)
| Activity | Significant hazard | Control (hierarchy tier) — collective before personal | Owner | Residual |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Partition demolition (level 3) | Collapse / dust / falling debris | Sequenced soft-strip + exclusion zone (Engineering/Admin); RPE last (PPE) | Demolition Supervisor | Medium |
| Steel-frame erection & cladding (levels 3–5) | Fall from height at the leading edge | **Eliminate** at-height work by ground-level pre-assembly where possible; **collective** edge protection + MEWP + nets (Engineering); personal fall-arrest only where collective is not reasonably practicable, justified (PPE) | Works Supervisor | High — hold-point until edge protection signed off |
| Lifting cladding panels | Load failure / strike over the footpath | Appointed-person lift plan + exclusion zone + no lift over occupied/public areas (Engineering/Admin) | Appointed Person | Medium |

### Work-at-height control — the hierarchy gate (the site team's draft was "operatives to wear harnesses")
The draft control "operatives to wear harnesses" is **PPE-led and is rejected as the headline
control**. Pushed up the hierarchy:
1. **Elimination** — pre-assemble cladding sub-frames at ground level to remove at-height work.
2. **Engineering (collective — LEADS)** — double-guardrail edge protection + toe-boards to every
   open leading edge; MEWP access; safety nets beneath.
3. **Administrative** — permit-to-load, inspected anchor points, exclusion zone beneath.
4. **PPE (justified last resort)** — personal fall-arrest (harness + lanyard) **only** where
   collective protection is not reasonably practicable, and justified — never the headline.

> **Collective protection leads; a harness-only work-at-height control is not acceptable as the
> headline.**
