## Duty basis (stated up front)
Under **CDM 2015 Regulation 12(5)** the **principal designer** prepares and maintains this
Health & Safety File. Under **Reg 12(6)–(9)** it is reviewed and updated through the project,
**handed to the client at completion**, and the client retains it and makes it available to
anyone who needs it for future construction, maintenance, cleaning, refurbishment, or
demolition.

## 1. Structure description & use
Tower B, levels 3–9 office fit-out; occupied commercial use. RC frame with non-load-bearing
infill; level-9 atrium and roof plant deck.
