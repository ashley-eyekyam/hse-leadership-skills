## The construction activity

Excavate a **1.8 m deep service trench** across the car-park access road to lay a new
water main. The access road remains live to occasional vehicle traffic; pedestrians
cross at one point.

## Sequence of works

1. Mobilise; CAT-and-Genny scan and trial-hole to locate buried services.
2. Set up traffic management and a pedestrian diversion.
3. Break out the road surface.
4. Excavate the trench; install trench support (shoring box).
5. Lay the main and backfill in layers.
6. Reinstate the surface; remove traffic management.
