## The construction activity (specificity anchor #1)

A **tandem lift** of precast concrete beams (two mobile cranes lifting one beam in
unison) to place them onto a building's frame at first-floor level. The lift area is
within the active building site; other trades are nearby.

## Sequence of works (specificity anchor #2)

1. Plan and brief the lift; establish the exclusion zone and tag lines.
2. Position and set up both mobile cranes on prepared ground.
3. Attach the lifting accessories; proof the rigging.
4. Perform the tandem lift under the appointed person's direction.
5. Land and secure the beam; release the rigging.
6. Stand down the lift; clear the exclusion zone.

## Plant & equipment

Two mobile cranes; lifting accessories (slings, shackles, spreader beam); tag lines.
