# RAMS request — erect mobile tower & replace south-elevation cladding (UK / CDM)

De-identification pass complete BEFORE drafting. No personal identifiers are present
in this brief; personnel are referred to by role label. The competent persons for the
sign-off record are to be assigned by the principal contractor at briefing.

## Jurisdiction & role

- Jurisdiction: **UK**. Construction (Design and Management) Regulations 2015 apply.
- Role (CDM): **principal contractor**.

## The construction activity (specificity anchor #1)

Erect a mobile access tower to replace cladding panels on the **south elevation,
levels 2–4** of an occupied office building. A public footpath runs alongside the
south elevation; other trades are working on adjacent elevations.

## Sequence of works (specificity anchor #2)

1. Mobilise and set up an exclusion zone on the public footpath.
2. Inspect and erect the mobile alloy tower.
3. Transfer materials to the work level.
4. Remove the old cladding panels.
5. Position a MEWP and fit the new panels.
6. Inspect the completed work.
7. Dismantle the tower.
8. Clear the site and stand down the exclusion zone.

## Plant & equipment

Mobile alloy tower; MEWP (boom/scissor); gin wheel / hoist; hand tools.

## Personnel & competencies

- Tower erection: a **PASMA-trained** operative.
- MEWP operation: an **IPAF-certified** operator.
- Site supervision: an SSSTS/SMSTS supervisor.
