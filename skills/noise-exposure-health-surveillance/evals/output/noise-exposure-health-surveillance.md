## 3. Noise-reduction plan (hierarchy-ranked)

Per `KB-SNIP-HOC` + `KB-SNIP-NOISE-CONTROL-HIERARCHY` — **hearing protection is LAST**:

| Control | Tier | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Retrofit a quieter die-cushion + damp the press frame (source) | Engineering | Maintenance Lead |
| Acoustic enclosure / barrier around the line-2 press | Engineering | Engineering Manager |
| Job rotation + time-limited exposure for the operator SEG | Administrative | Production Lead |
| Issue + fit-test hearing protection (HPD) — LAST resort | PPE | HSE Lead |

> **Hearing protection alone does not satisfy the hierarchy.** For the ≥ 85 dBA SEGs the
> source/engineering controls above are assessed first; an "issue ear defenders" only program
> would be flagged PPE-led.

## 4. Hearing-conservation-program elements

- SEGs ≥ 85 dBA enrolled in the hearing-conservation program.
- Mandatory hearing-protection zones signed for the ≥ 90 dBA press area.
- Annual noise-awareness training + HPD fit-testing.
- Re-survey on any process / equipment change.
