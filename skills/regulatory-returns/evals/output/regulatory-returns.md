## 2. Recordability determination — the test applied (OSHA 1904.7)

The incident facts (Q3): a despatch-bay laceration; the worker received **a single
adhesive dressing at the on-site first-aid station — no sutures**, **no work
restriction**, **returned to the same job the same shift**.

| 1904 criterion | Applied to the facts | Result |
|---|---|---|
| Work-related (1904.5) | Occurred in the despatch bay during work | Yes |
| New case (1904.6) | First presentation of this injury | Yes |
| General recording criterion (1904.7) | Treatment was an **adhesive dressing only** — on the **first-aid list at 1904.7(b)(5)(ii)** (using non-prescription bandages / dressings); **no sutures**, no medical treatment beyond first aid; **no days away, no restricted work / job transfer**, no loss of consciousness | **No** |

**Verdict: NOT recordable.** A first-aid-only case (adhesive dressing, no sutures, no
days-away/restricted work) does **not** meet a general recording criterion under
**1904.7**. **Logging it on the 300 was an error.**

[GAP] If a fact changes the picture — e.g. the wound was actually sutured, or restricted
work followed — re-run the test; the verdict turns on the treatment + outcome facts.

## 3. Correct outcome + form

- **Remove the erroneous 300 log entry** for this case — there is **no 300 obligation
  for a first-aid-only case**.
- No **301** incident report is required for a non-recordable case (the 301 pairs with a
  recordable 300 entry).
- Keep the first-aid record internally per the org's procedure (not an OSHA recordkeeping
  obligation).

## 4. Deadline (jurisdiction-anchored)

Because the case is **not recordable**, there is **no 300/300A/electronic-submission
deadline triggered** by it. For reference, had it been recordable: the **300 entry** is
due within **7 calendar days** of learning of the case (1904.29(b)(3)); the **300A**
annual summary is posted **Feb 1 – Apr 30, 2026** for CY2025; **electronic 300A
submission (1904.41)** applies only if the establishment's size/SIC is in scope.
**No deadline is asserted without the jurisdiction.**
