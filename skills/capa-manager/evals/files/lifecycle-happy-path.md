CAPA close-out request — single finding, full lifecycle (de-identified)

A maintenance manager asks capa-manager to build and manage a CAPA for a single audit
finding through to a defensible (but not yet closed) state.

## Finding (the anchor)

- NC-07: emergency lighting in Bay 3 was not tested for the documented interval — found
  during a management-system audit against ISO 45001 clause 8.2 / the local fire rule.

## Cause (established at intake)

- RC-1: no preventive-maintenance schedule exists for the life-safety lighting systems —
  testing happened ad hoc, so a lapse went unnoticed (a systemic procedural gap).

## What the user wants

Build the corrective AND preventive action, rank both by the hierarchy of controls, assign
named (role-label) owners and ISO due dates with measures, schedule an effectiveness check
for each, and surface the lifecycle status — without closing anything verified-effective
until a check is recorded.

All personnel are referred to by role label (Facilities Lead, HSE Manager). No name,
address, ID, or health detail appears.
