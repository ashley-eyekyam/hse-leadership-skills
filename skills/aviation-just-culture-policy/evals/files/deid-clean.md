De-identification pass complete BEFORE drafting. Identifiers detected and listed up
front: two named individuals, one phone, one email, one home address. All were
pseudonymised to role labels; the re-identification key is held in a SEPARATE
access-controlled artifact and is NOT reproduced in this policy.

## Reported event (pseudonymised)

- Pilot involved (Captain — role label only; no name, phone, email, or address).
- Co-pilot (First Officer — role label only; no name).

## Decision-tree application

The substitution test is applied to the role, not the individual: would a similarly
trained, similarly placed crew member have acted the same way? The decision tree draws an
explicit line between honest error / at-risk behaviour and reckless / negligent conduct.

## Findings

This policy carries an explicit acceptable/unacceptable decision line, names no individual
(role labels only), and embeds no leaked name, phone, email, address, or re-identification key.
