## The five clause-5.2 commitments (context-fit to AcmeCo's real risks)

1. Framework for OH&S objectives — derived from forklift-traffic and solvent-exposure risk.
2. Fulfil legal and other requirements — UK HSWA duties + ISO 45001 certification scope.
3. Eliminate hazards and reduce risk — segregate pedestrian/forklift routes (engineering)
   and substitute lower-VOC solvents, before any reliance on PPE.
4. Continual improvement of the OH&S management system.
5. Consultation and participation of workers via the site safety committee.
